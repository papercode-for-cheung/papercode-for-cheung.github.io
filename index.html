    • Research Interests

    AI for Science, Medical Informatics
  • Medical Informatics: We study NLP and CV tasks involved in medical informatics, such as drug repositioning, medical image processing, biomedical named entity recognition, clinical text mining, etc., and construct neural network models for the task characteristics and application scenarios. Relevant papers have been published in journals and conferences, such as ESWA, BIBM, EAAI, etc.
  • AI for Science: We have carried out research on core substructure identification, material property analysis and prediction, and lithium battery failure knowledge mapping for scientific problems in materials science and constructed AI methods for multi-dimensional molecular structure representation learning. Relevant papers have been published in journals and conferences, such as DASFAA, etc.
